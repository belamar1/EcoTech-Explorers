About EcoTech Explorers

EcoTech Explorers is a project of Secure Things Innova Ltd, dedicated to fostering curiosity and innovation among children aged 7-15. Our goal is to build future problem solvers who are equipped to tackle environmental challenges through hands-on projects and personalized AI-assisted learning experiences.

Our Programs

Our programs immerse children in a world of curiosity and innovation, building their abilities and confidence. We provide workshops through schools, clubs, and groups such as Scouts. Parents can enroll their children in these workshops, and we also welcome sponsorships to support our mission.

Join EcoTech Explorers

We offer three types of programs to cater to different needs:

  • Virtual Programs: Participate from anywhere with internet access.
  • In-Person Programs: Join our workshops at various locations.
  • In-Person Programs with Financial Aid: Available for families needing financial assistance.

Virtual Programs

Our virtual programs allow children to participate in our workshops from the comfort of their homes. These programs are designed to be interactive and engaging, utilizing online tools and resources to deliver a high-quality learning experience.

In-Person Programs

Our in-person programs are conducted at various locations, providing hands-on learning experiences. These workshops are led by experienced instructors and offer a more immersive learning environment.

In-Person Programs with Financial Aid

We believe that every child should have access to our programs, regardless of their financial situation. We offer financial aid for families who need assistance to enroll their children in our in-person workshops.

FAQ

Who can join EcoTech Explorers?
Our programs are designed for children aged 7-15. We also welcome educators, parents, and supporters to get involved in various capacities.
How do the programs work?
